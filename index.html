Who We Are

“Niriksha is a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering marginalized communities. Through our work in education, healthcare, livelihood, and social inclusion, we aim to break the cycle of poverty and create lasting change. Our vision is a world where everyone has the opportunity to thrive, regardless of their background or circumstance.”
