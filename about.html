About Us

"Niriksha Foundation was established in 2022, Established with the belief that every individual deserves dignity, opportunity, and hope, Niriksha works tirelessly to bridge gaps in access to essential resources and opportunities.

Our programs focus on empowering children with quality education, providing healthcare access to marginalized families, and equipping women with the skills and resources they need to achieve economic independence and lead their communities. Sustainability is at the heart of our mission, ensuring that our efforts not only create immediate relief but also foster long-term resilience and growth.

Through community-driven and collaborative approaches, Niriksha has positively impacted thousands of lives. From establishing schools and health camps to promoting gender equality and environmental conservation, our work addresses the root causes of poverty and inequality.

At Niriksha Foundation, we believe in the power of collective action to create a brighter future. By partnering with individuals, organizations, and communities, we aim to build an inclusive and equitable society where every person has the tools to thrive. Our Mission

To empower underserved communities by ensuring access to education, healthcare, and opportunities for personal growth while fostering sustainable development and equality.

Our Vision

To create a world where every individual has access to the resources and opportunities they need to live with dignity, break the cycle of poverty, and contribute to a thriving society.

Key Achievements

In 2022, conducted a vaccination camp, benefiting over 1,800 individuals during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Provided free education to 500 children in 2023, ensuring access to quality learning in underserved communities.

In 2024, reached 2,000+ people with health awareness campaigns on preventive healthcare and hygiene practices.

Conducted skill-building workshops for 300 youth in 2023, offering vocational training in various practical fields.

In 2022, planted 2,000 trees to promote environmental conservation and reduce the urban carbon footprint.

Empowered 500 women in 2024 with financial independence and entrepreneurship training through various skill-building programs.

Distributed over 10,000 meals to migrant workers and low-income families during the lockdown in 2023.

In 2025, organized sanitation campaigns in slums, providing hygiene kits to 1,000 families in need.

Offered free health check-ups, diagnostic tests, and medications to 1,500 individuals in low-income areas in 2024.

In 2023, raised awareness on child protection, benefiting 2,000+ children through policy advocacy and community programs.
