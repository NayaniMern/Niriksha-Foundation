Programs

Education Programs

"Shiksha Jyoti" (Lighting the Path to Education)

Education is at the heart of our mission to break the cycle of poverty. Niriksha is committed to providing access to quality education for children and youth from underserved communities. Through our education programs, we focus on bridging the gap between inequality and opportunity.

Key Initiatives:

  • Community Learning Centers: We run after-school centers that provide supplementary education, homework assistance, and access to learning materials for children in rural and underprivileged areas.
  • Scholarship Programs: We provide scholarships to deserving students who would otherwise be unable to afford education, ensuring they can continue their studies without financial barriers.
  • Teacher Training: We train local teachers to improve the quality of education in their communities by equipping them with modern teaching techniques and tools.
  • Digital Literacy: In today’s world, digital literacy is essential. Our programs help students and adults learn to use computers, the internet, and other digital tools to gain access to information and opportunities.

Healthcare Programs

"Swasthya Disha" (Path to Health and Wellness)

Access to quality healthcare is a fundamental right that many communities are deprived of. Niriksha’s healthcare programs focus on preventive care, awareness, and basic medical support to improve health outcomes for underserved populations

Key Initiatives:

  • Mobile Health Camps: We conduct free health camps in remote and underserved areas, offering medical consultations, health screenings, and essential medications to those who otherwise lack access to healthcare services.
  • Awareness Campaigns: We run health education campaigns focusing on topics like hygiene, nutrition, maternal health, and disease prevention to equip communities with the knowledge they need to stay healthy.
  • Mental Health Support: Mental health is often overlooked, especially in marginalized communities. Our programs provide counseling services and workshops to raise awareness and provide support for mental well-being
  • COVID-19 Relief Programs: During the pandemic, we provided essential medical supplies, health education, and COVID-19 testing services to vulnerable populations.

Skill Development & Livelihood Programs

"Udyog Jyoti" (Light of Livelihood)

Building sustainable livelihoods is essential for individuals to become self-reliant. Niriksha provides skill development programs designed to empower women, youth, and marginalized groups with practical skills that open doors to better employment opportunities.

Key Initiatives:

  • Entrepreneurship Programs Through workshops and mentorship, we help individuals start their own businesses by providing guidance on business planning, financial management, and marketing strategies.
  • Women Empowerment: We focus on providing economic independence to women by offering training in skills such as embroidery, crafts, and small-scale agriculture, along with financial literacy education to help them manage and grow their income.
  • Vocational Training Centers: We focus on providing economic independence to women by offering training in skills such as embroidery, crafts, and small-scale agriculture, along with financial literacy education to help them manage and grow their income.

Women & Child Welfare Programs

"Sakhi Shakti" (Empowering Women and Children)

At Niriksha, we prioritize the well-being and protection of women and children, who are often the most vulnerable members of society. Our programs aim to empower women, reduce gender inequality, and improve the lives of children in marginalized communities.

Key Initiatives:

  • Women’s Health and Safety: We provide health services, legal awareness, and self-defense training to help women protect their rights and improve their quality of life.
  • Child Protection and Education: Our programs focus on ensuring that children have access to education, healthcare, and a safe environment. We run child protection awareness campaigns and create safe spaces where children can learn and grow.
  • Nutrition and Care for Mothers and Children: We provide nutritional support, especially for pregnant women and infants, to ensure that they receive adequate food and healthcare to thrive.
  • Advocacy for Women’s Rights: We engage in advocacy efforts to raise awareness about women’s rights and promote policies that ensure gender equality and protection for women and children.
