Volunteers

At Niriksha Foundation, we believe in the power of people. Volunteers play a crucial role in our mission, and your support can make a world of difference. Whether you're looking to make a direct impact through hands-on work or contribute your skills behind the scenes, there are numerous opportunities to get involved.

As a volunteer, you will join a passionate and committed team of individuals working to create positive change in communities. Volunteering with us allows you to use your skills, time, and energy to help build a better future for those in need.

Niriskha Foundation seeks volunteers to help train resource-poor individuals to become confident and independent. Volunteer roles include teaching computer skills, sewing, content writing, web management, and translation services. Discover more about their volunteer opportunities:
